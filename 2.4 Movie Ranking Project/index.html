The Best Movie according to Neelx

My top 3 movies of all time.

Django Unchained

Django, an African slave, is freed by a German bounty hunter and becomes his apprentice. Together, they attempt to rescue his wife, who has been enslaved by a charming but cruel plantation owner.


Fight Club

Unhappy with his capitalistic lifestyle, a white-collared insomniac forms an underground fight club with Tyler, a careless soap salesman. Soon, their venture spirals down into something sinister.


Ocean's Thirteen

Danny and the gang have only one reason to pull off their most risky heist, to defend one of their own, but they're going to need more than luck to break the ruthless Willy and his casino.
